Version 3

About in Long

Lochross exists for organisations that have grown around spreadsheets, inboxes, and workarounds. The systems usually “work”, but they cost hours every week and create avoidable friction. I focus on turning that into one clear workflow that staff can actually trust and use.

My work is intentionally lightweight. Instead of imposing a heavy platform, I start with workflow mapping and identify where time, quality, or accountability is leaking. Then I design and build a focused system around your real process. Recent examples include a local-hosted purchase order and approval workflow with audit-ready storage, report automation with consistent outputs, and admin automation for charities handling complex funding cycles.

Each project is delivered end-to-end: discovery, build, testing, rollout, and support. You get practical documentation and direct handover so the system keeps working when day-to-day pressure returns. Long term, the direction is local-first tooling where appropriate, reducing SaaS sprawl while preserving data ownership.

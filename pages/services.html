What I Offer

Services

Workflow Discovery & Scoping

We map how work currently moves through your team, identify bottlenecks, and define the smallest useful system to fix them. Output is a clear implementation plan with scope and expected outcomes.

Custom Internal Tools

I build lightweight software for repetitive admin and document-heavy workflows. Examples include purchase order approvals, report generation, form-driven document systems, and process tracking tools.

Implementation & Rollout Support

Delivery includes testing, handover documentation, and practical support during rollout. The goal is real adoption, not just deployment.

Local-First System Direction

Where appropriate, systems are designed to reduce SaaS sprawl and keep your operational data under your own control.
